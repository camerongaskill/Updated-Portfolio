Hi! I'm Cameron Gaskill

I’m a student over at Thinkful studying Software Engineering. I am 18 years old born in Boston, MA, and I currently live in Charlotte, NC. After being laid off due to COVID-19, I decided to make a career change from the welding industry into a field of tech.

After graduating, I plan to move back to my hometown of Boston and begin my career as a software engineer, later on, I hope to specialize in cybersecurity with a rule such as a pen tester or possibly a security architect. I find the rapidly growing world of technology very exciting and I’m ready to begin my journey of life-long learning.

About Me

After going through a number of introductory coding classes in high school, I was hooked on the endless wonders and possibilities of coding, the free-range for a developer’s creativity, and the ability of a whole team to make great changes in the industry. Even so soon in my short experience, the small victories I have from figuring out a small project show how perfect the field is for me.

The most surprising part from the perspective of a newcomer developer is the amount of teamwork that takes place, the interaction between different, yet similarly minded people. Seeing each developer’s personality in their code is very interesting, and it feels amazing to work together on a task, getting insight from others you would’ve never had.

Outside of coding I love being active; working out, hiking, camping, boxing, wrestling, hockey, you name it, I love to do it. One of my main passions is helping coach my alma mater’s wrestling team. I love the dynamics of both teamwork and being fully responsible on your own. I’ve currently been wrestling for about 10 years and have practiced Brazilian jiu-jitsu for 7 years.

The service I hope to provide for my clients is to give each of them the Magnum Opus of web development. I want to create my clients the most tailored fit websites, with great care going into both the fron and backend of the site. If you’re looking for someone who’s not afraid of a challenge or someone who can help you with a small task, whatever it is, I’m the right person. I love to take hold of any opportunity I can to learn and perfect my craft, as management consultant Peter Drucker said, “Knowledge has to be improved, challenged, and increased constantly, or it vanishes”.
